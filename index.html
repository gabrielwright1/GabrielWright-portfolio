About Me

I am a front-end developer with a background automating inefficient processes.

Despite the constraints of a legacy enterprise environment, I regularly found ways to save users more than $2,000 per/month by building workflow-powered web applications with SharePoint, Nintex, and InfoPath. By working closely with subject matter experts, I built solutions that were tested, documented, and deployed to 4 separate lines of business at Hydro One Networks Inc.

Over the last #1000DaysOfCode, I've been studying on evenings and weekends, building projects, and graduating from web development bootcamp. You can see what I'm working on by visiting my LinkedIn / Twitter.
